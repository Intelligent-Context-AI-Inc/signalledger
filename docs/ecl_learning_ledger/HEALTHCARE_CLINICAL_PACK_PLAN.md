# Healthcare/Clinical Pack Plan

## Why Domain #2

Healthcare/Clinical is the strongest next pack because it proves ECL can serve high-compliance AI teams while preserving the no-payload boundary.

## Source Families

- ClinicalTrials.gov metadata.
- NIH public metadata descriptors.
- FDA public label and device metadata.
- Public clinical benchmark cards.
- Synthetic-data guidance and evaluation summaries.
- Medical taxonomy descriptors such as MeSH and public coding-system references.

## Allowed Metadata

- Study phase tags.
- Trial category tags.
- Source-reference hashes.
- License/provenance descriptors.
- De-identification method tags.
- Benchmark aliases.
- Aggregate modality and source-family ratios.

## Not Allowed

- Clinical notes.
- Patient records.
- Case narratives.
- Prompts or completions.
- Raw trial descriptions.
- Embeddings.
- Token sequences.
- Model weights.

## First Pack Milestone

Create six metadata-only seed records, one benchmark alias registry, one synthetic-data risk profile, one no-payload red-team fixture family, and one sanitized compliance-passport example.
