# Compliance Positioning

`ecl-trainer` supports evidence generation for training-data governance. It helps teams create metadata-only artifacts for transparency, technical documentation, internal audit, and release review workflows.

The SDK does not guarantee legal compliance, certify dataset legality, replace counsel, or determine whether a model may be publicly released. Legal, privacy, IP, and governance review remain required before public model release.

## Supported Profiles

- `AB2013_PUBLIC_SUMMARY`: high-level training-data transparency support artifact.
- `EU_AI_ACT_TECHNICAL_DOC`: technical documentation support artifact.
- `INTERNAL_AUDIT`: internal provenance, license, risk, and hash-chain evidence.
- `MODEL_RELEASE_SIGNOFF`: model release review packet.
- `LEGAL_EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY`: plain-language evidence summary for legal and risk teams.

## Evidence Artifacts

Reports may include source-root hashes, content hashes, schema hashes, provenance descriptors, license descriptors, synthetic-data indicators, mutation trail metadata, evaluation linkage, risk flags, and hash-chain verification.

## Claim Boundaries

Use: “supports evidence generation,” “supports transparency workflows,” and “supports technical documentation workflows.”

Avoid: “guarantees compliance,” “certifies legal dataset use,” “approves model release,” or “replaces legal review.”
